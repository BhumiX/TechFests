Welcome to TechFest 2025, the largest celebration of technology, creativity, and innovation. This 3-day event brings together students, professionals, creators, and tech enthusiasts from all around the country. From inspiring keynote speeches to hands-on workshops, TechFest promises to deliver an unforgettable experience filled with knowledge and excitement.

This year’s theme focuses on AI, Robotics, Web Development, Cybersecurity, and Future Tech. Attendees will have the chance to explore the latest advancements, interact with industry experts, and participate in engaging activities designed for all experience levels.
